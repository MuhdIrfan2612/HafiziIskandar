   CORPORATE PROFILE  
   
 

DELTA FORCE SECURITY SERVICES & CONSULTANCY SDN BHD, registered under Malaysian Home Ministry and Finance Ministry began life in 1997. The establishment of our corporate entity is well accepeted by the security Services Association of Malaysia (Persatuan Perkhidmatan Kawalan Keselamatan Malaysia / PPKKM) as its member.

Having gained wide experience in the local security industry, its principals saw an opportunity to provide customers with a service that was not only responsive and reliable but ready to deliver real value for money in an increasingly competitive market place.

This vision still underpins our service today, DELTA FORCE has grown by 'delivering the goods' to clients throughout Malaysia. We are the only security provider to back our service provision with this promise;

Where ever you are in Malaysia you can be sure of a 100% full security services be it at home, work or just about anywhere.

DELTA FORCE SECURITY SERVICES & CONSULTANCY SDN BHD can offer the solution that your organisation is seeking, at a price meets your budget.

FULLY TRAINED & QUALIFIED PERSONNEL OF INTEGRITY

Prior to employment, DELTA FORCE ensures that all aspiring security assistants and officers meets its stringent standard criteria for recruitment and pass the mandated pre-employment security vetting by the POLICE to maintain the desired integrity.

The security assistants and officers comprises of young matured able bodied career minded individuals who possess at least the minimum academic qualification of having successfully completed secondary education.

They are mandated to pass the full medical screening and undergo a probationary period of at least three months prior to confirmation of employment. Additionally, they are also mandated to undergo one week of basic training to enable them to exhibit a standard security identification smart card issued by the Ministry if Home Affairs (KDN).

Our highly experienced, competent and qualified trainers ensures that they are effectively trained and customised accordingly to meet the stringent professional standards desirable for effective government, industrial, residential and commercial security encompassing the physical and methodological aspects of protection/prevention, first aid and basic fire fighting and rescue.
