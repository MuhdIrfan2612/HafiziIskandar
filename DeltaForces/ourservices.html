   OUR SERVICES  
   
 

DELTA FORCE SECURITY SERVICES & CONSULTANCY SDN BHD, registered under Malaysia Ministry of Finance (Kementerian Kewangan Malaysia), began life in 1998. The establishment of our corporate entity is well accepted by the Security Services Association Of Malaysia (Persatuan Perkhidmatan Kawalan Keselamatan Malaysia / PPKKM) as its member.

Our services includes the following;
 
         
  SECURITY GUARDS   NEPALESE GUARD   PRIVATE INVESTIGATIONS   MOBILE PATROLS  
                 
           
  GUARD DOGS   PERSONAL BODYGUARD   SECURITY SYSTEMS
