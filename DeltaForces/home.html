DELTA FORCE SECURITY SERVICES & CONSULTANCY SDN BHD, registered under Malaysia Ministry of Finance (Kementerian Kewangan Malaysia), began life in 1997. The establishment of our corporate entity is well accepted by the Security Services Association Of Malaysia (Persatuan Perkhidmatan Kawalan Keselamatan Malaysia / PPKKM) as its member.

The company is spearheaded by our Managing Director, Pn. Fazilah Abdul Kader, who has vast experience in Financial and Management as well as Security Operational Management. Also, a Diploma holder in Business Management and a Degree in Business Studies.

Having gained wide experience in the local security industry, its principals saw an opportunity to provide customers with a service that was not only responsive and reliable but ready to deliver real value-for-money in an increasingly competitive marketplace.
